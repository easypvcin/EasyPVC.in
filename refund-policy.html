Refund & Return Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

1. Eligibility for Refund & Replacement

At Print420, we strive to provide the best quality printed products. However, due to the custom nature of our products, refunds and replacements are only applicable under the following conditions:

  • ✅ Misprint or Printing Defect: If the product you received has printing errors or is damaged due to our mistake.
  • ✅ Damaged on Delivery: If the product arrives damaged, you must report it within 3 days of delivery with supporting photos/videos.

⚠ No refunds/replacements will be provided for:

  • ❌ Incorrect uploads or mistakes made by the customer.
  • ❌ Color variation due to screen differences.
  • ❌ Delay in delivery due to external factors (courier issues, weather conditions, etc.).
  • ❌ Orders placed by mistake or a change of mind after printing has started.

2. How to Request a Refund or Replacement

If you meet the eligibility criteria, follow these steps:

  1. Report Within 3 Days: Email us at pvcprint420@gmail.com with:
    • Order ID
    • Photos/Videos showing the misprint or damage.
    • Detailed Description of the issue.
  2. Verification Process: Our team will review the case within 3-5 business days.
  3. Approval/Rejection Notification: If approved, you will receive:
    • ✅ Replacement: A new print will be sent at no extra cost.
    • ✅ Refund: If applicable, it will be processed within 7-10 business days to your original payment method.

4. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven’t received your refund yet, please:

  • ✅ Reach out to your bank (some processing delays occur).
  • ✅ If the issue persists, email us at pvcprint420@gmail.com.

5. Exchange Policy

We only replace items if they are:

  • ✅ Defective or
  • ✅ Damaged during printing or delivery

How to Request an Exchange:

📩 Email pvcprint420@gmail.com with proof of the issue within 3 days of delivery.

6. Gift Orders

🎁 If an item was marked as a gift and shipped directly to you:

If the item wasn’t marked as a gift, the refund will be issued to the original buyer.
